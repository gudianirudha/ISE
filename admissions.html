Eligibility Requirement

 

Admission to 1st semester B.E. / B.Arch

A) Through Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA)

For admissions to 1st year In qualifying examination, candidates should have In CET-Examination
Engineering (B.E.) Passed in 2nd PUC / 12th Std and obtained a minimum of 45% of marks in aggregate in PCM No minimum marks prescribed in CET
Architecture (B.Arch.) Passed in 2ndPassed in 2nd PUC / 12th Std and obtained a minimum of 50% of marks in aggregate in PCMB Minimum of 40% of marks in NATA for all categories

Eligibility for Management quota is same as CET

For more details please visit http://kea.kar.nic.in

 

B) Through COMED-K

The COMED-K entrance test,

For more details please visit : http://www.comedk.org/
